# Driving Experience & Spatial Navigation Performance (n=694)

[![Paper](https://img.shields.io/badge/Journal-Spatial_Cognition_&_Computation-blue)](https://doi.org/10.1080/13875868.2024.2319735)

This repository contains the analysis scripts for the study: 
**"Individuals learning to drive solo before age 18 have superior spatial navigation ability compared with those who learn later"** *(Published in Spatial Cognition & Computation, 2024)*.

## 🔬 Key Finding
- **Early Acquisition Advantage:** Participants who began driving solo **before age 18** demonstrated significantly better wayfinding performance on Sea Hero Quest compared to those who started at or after age 18.
- **Critical Window:** The results suggest that independent navigation during late adolescence may be a key period for the development or refinement of spatial cognitive maps.

## 📁 Files
- `Rcodedrivingpaper.R`: Regression models and data cleaning scripts.
- `Edtravel.ipynb`: Visualizations of navigation scores across different age-of-onset cohorts.
  
## 🛠 Skills Demonstrated
- **Multiple Linear Regression:** Controlling for age, gender, and other demographics to isolate the effect of driving onset.
- **Categorical Data Analysis:** Handling and modeling self-report experiential data (driving age, frequency, habits).
- **Behavioral Modeling:** Linking real-world life milestones (obtaining a license) to digital performance metrics.
